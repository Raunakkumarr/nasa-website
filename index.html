About us

Welcome to NASA

NASA International College (NASA), an exclusive center of +2 education under the ownership of NASA International Education Network, prepares individuals for a comprehensive scientific and technological journey. By nurturing intellectual curiosity and fostering a passion for exploration, NASA empowers its students to become valuable assets in the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Meet Our teachers
