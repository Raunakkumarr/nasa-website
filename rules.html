Code of Conduct

NASA NATIONAL SECONDARY SCHOOL

Tinkune, Kathmandu
Commitment Letter

It is a well-known fact that academic achievement and excellence of an institution depend upon regularity, punctuality and discipline of its students. Discipline is the back bone of development and success, our thrust is solely focused on quality education.
The norms and regulations formulated by the college management must be followed by all the students of the college. Violation of these norms and regulations would result in the punishments or fines and even expulsion from college depending on the nature of violations.
We understand that every student of NASA is responsible, disciplined and honorable; they are responsible for uplifting the image of the college in every way possible. The students must follow the codes of conduct or rules and regulations mentioned in the commitment letter, which are as follows.

  • Discipline codes:
    • The students must be polite, respectful and behave responsibly. Irresponsible and impolite behavior will be considered as an act of indiscipline. Rude behavior or misconduct may be considered as a sufficient reason for expulsion.
    • The students must switch off the mobile phone in the College. In emergency, they can use mobile phone only after the consultation/permission of coordinator or Academic Director. If the students are caught using/talking/ chatting on mobile phone in the college premises, they will be fined Rs. 200/- on first offence, Rs. 500/- on second offence and on repeated offences the mobile will be seized and will not be returned until the parent/guardian contacts college authority. All fined amount will be transferred to student welfare fund. The rule is same for any electronic devices. Further, College will not be responsible for any lost, stolen or damaged devices.
    • The students found smoking, chewing tobacco and chewing gum in the college premises or inside the college bus and anywhere in college bus and anywhere in college uniform will be suspended and their parents/ guardians will be notified. Repetition of the same act will result in expulsion from the college.
    • The students are warned for their misconducts. In case of repetition of misconducts, parents/ guardians will be notified. But in case of gross violation such as possession, use and paddling of drugs, alcohol, stealing or damaging the college property, vandalism, criminal cases etc., the college management reserves the right to expel the defaulter without explanation or written notice in advance.
    • Forming groups and getting involved in unhealthy competition are not allowed in NASA. Fighting with the friends and showing rude behavior with teachers inside or outside the college invite serious corrective measures. In such cases, students will be suspended.
    • Actions effecting the reputation of the college are considered as a serious offence and such an act results in the expulsion.
    • Students have to be in the classroom before the concerned faculty enters the class. Regularity in studies is a must. Students failing to submit assignments on time or not appearing for class test will be fined Rs. 200/-. Repetitive negligence in tests and assignments would result in parents/guardians being summoned by the respective teachers.
  • Uniform codes:
    • Dress code must be maintained properly. Each and every student must be in cleaned and ironed college uniform along with simple black leather- shoes with laces. The bottom of the pants should not be less than 16". The students are not allowed to wears pants below waist and above the ankles.
    • The students are strictly restricted from attending parties, visiting restaurants and going on picnics (except those organized by college) in the college uniform. If found violating this rule, the management reserves the right to suspend or expel them.
    • Boys are not permitted to have long and clumsy hair. Ear rings/ bangles, heavy makeup such as hair styling, long nail polishing and lipstick are not allowed for girls.
    • Students must wear the v-neck sweater with college logo and coat in the winter session. Out dress Pole-neck/ round sweaters, v-neck cardigan, jackets etc. are not allowed.
  • Attendance-cum-timing Codes:
    • Without prior written application or without prior telephone call by parents/ guardians, the absentees must pay Rs. 100. The fined amount will be transferred to student welfare fund.
    • In case of unforeseen medical or emergency leave, the students must bring application with letter from parent or doctorâ€™s prescription when they come to college later.
    • The students who are absent even a single day must inform the college administration and submit a letter of application explaining the reason of absence from their parents/ guardians. The students who remain absent without the information/consent of college administration for more than 15 days are subject to expulsion from the college.
    • The management does not tolerate late coming. The students have to report to the college 10 minutes prior to their classes.
  • Examination Codes:
    • The college conducts three terminal examinations. If a student remains absent in the terminal examination without any valid reason and those who fail in the terminal examination must appear in re-test for which a sum of Rs. 500 will be charged per subject as a fine.
    • Parents/ guardians are expected to monitor the progress of their children and must accompany their wards to the college on the day of Report Card Distribution to collect the results of terminal examination and interact with faculty members for the improvement of their wards.
    • The students must score at least 40 percent marks in send-up to be qualified to fill the Board Examination Form.
    • The students must maintain a disciplined environment during the examination. Using unfair means would result in serious consequences.
  • Financial Codes:
    • The amount of fees related to each installment has to be paid within the given period. In case of failure to do so the student has to pay late fine.
    • Those who get admitted at the hostel should pay the 1st installment before entering the hostel.
I, hereby, thoroughly read all the aforesaid norms and regulations and heartily commit to abide by them. In case of violation of these codes, I accept any disciplinary action taken by the college administration.
