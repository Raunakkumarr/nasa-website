Scholarships

Scholarship Scheme
This scheme is a motivation, reward and advantage for deserving, potential, sincere and needy students. It aims to encourage, motivate and value deserving students and lessen financial burden on their parents. The deserving students are provided with different types of scholarships: partial, full or other types, depending on different categories or criteria. There is a special provision of scholarship scheme to SEE Board Toppers, District toppers and skilled sports players. Besides this, having realized the necessity of uplifting the status of Nepalese in different sectors, a special scholarship provision has been made for Dalits, Janajaties, handicapped, female students and the students of remote areas.
The scholarship thus awarded is effective for one academic session (Grade XI). The scholarship holder should obtain minimum Grade in each terminal examination. Likewise, the continuation of scholarship in Grade XII, depends on the student's result of Grade XI, NEB exam. Scholarship status of the student may change in grade XII depending upon their performance. The ones who got the scholarship in Grade XI may lose in grade XI and the ones who failed to get scholarship in Grade XI may win in grade XII. Thus, there is the possibility for every deserving student to get the scholarship.
